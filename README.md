# Trading Learning

A personal, self-paced curriculum for learning to trade — starting with the **Indian stock market (NSE) and intraday trading**, with later stages planned for commodities, currencies, and global/foreign markets.

> ⚠️ Everything in this repository is for **education only**, not financial advice. All prices and numbers in examples are hypothetical. Markets carry real risk of loss — never trade money you cannot afford to lose.

## The staged plan

1. **Indian equity market + intraday trading** ← current focus
2. Commodities
3. Currencies
4. Foreign / global markets

This repo currently covers **Stage 1** in full: a 12-week, week-by-week course to go from zero to a first real (small) intraday trade on the NSE.

## Where to start

Open **[docs/lessons/README.md](./docs/lessons/README.md)** and begin with **Week 1**. Each lesson page has Previous / Course Home / Next links at the top and bottom, so you can work through the whole course in sequence without ever coming back to this file.

If you want the bird's-eye view first, read **[docs/roadmap.md](./docs/roadmap.md)** — it lays out all 12 weeks, the non-negotiable risk rules, and the go-live checklist before it points you into the lessons.

## The shape of the course

- **Weeks 1–11** are paper trading only (simulated money) — market mechanics, candlesticks, support/resistance and supply/demand zones, indicators, the Opening Range Breakout setup, risk & position sizing, psychology, and reviewing your own journal.
- **Week 12** is the first small, real trade — placed only if the go-live checklist in the roadmap is honestly complete.

## Core principles carried through every lesson

- A stop-loss on every trade, decided before entry.
- Risk ≤ 1–2% of capital per trade.
- Reward:risk ≥ 1:2.
- Discipline and process are graded — not profit.
