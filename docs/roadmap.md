# 3-Month Indian Intraday Trading Roadmap

**Goal:** From zero to your first _real_ intraday trade on the NSE (Nifty & Bank Nifty) in 12 weeks.

You spend the first **11 weeks learning and paper-trading with zero money at risk**, so that by Week 12 you place a small, real trade with rules and a journal already in place.

|                     |                                          |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **Time commitment** | ~1 hr/day + live market watch            |
| **Weeks 1–11**      | Paper trading only (simulated money)     |
| **Week 12**         | Small real capital                       |
| **Real goal**       | A repeatable _process_ - not fast profit |

> ⚠️ **Educational content only — not financial advice.** Nothing in this roadmap is a trade recommendation or suggestion. All examples and numbers are illustrative and hypothetical. Always consult a registered financial advisor before trading or investing. Trade and invest at your own risk — no responsibility is accepted for any loss, damage, or incorrect/invalid trades.

## Your Weekly Rhythm (repeat every week)

- **Mon–Fri AM** - Watch the first hour live (9:15–10:15 IST). Just observe and name what you see.
- **Daily** - 30–45 min of that week's topic + Zerodha Varsity reading.
- **Every trade** - Log it in your journal: entry, stop, exit, reason, screenshot, feeling.
- **Weekend** - Review the week's journal. What worked? What broke a rule?

## Month 1 - Foundations & Reading Price

_Learn the language of the chart._

### Week 1 - How the market actually works

- **Learn:** NSE/BSE, Nifty 50 & Bank Nifty, SEBI, the broker's role. Order types (market, limit, stop-loss). MIS vs CNC. Long vs short. Bid / ask / spread / volume.
- **Do:** Open a demo account (Zerodha Kite / TradingView paper). Start Zerodha Varsity Module 1 - free. Set up your charting workspace with [tradingview-guide.md](./tradingview-guide.md).
- **Milestone:** Account open; you can explain bid, ask, spread & volume in your own words.

### Week 2 - Candlestick anatomy

- **Learn:** OHLC, body vs wick, green vs red, what each timeframe means (1 / 5 / 15 min). Why the 5-min chart is the intraday workhorse.
- **Do:** Watch Nifty & Bank Nifty on a 5-min chart 30 min daily. Say out loud what each candle means.
- **Milestone:** Spot a doji and a long-wick rejection candle live, without help.

### Week 3 - Candlestick patterns that matter

- **Learn:** Hammer & shooting star, bullish/bearish engulfing, marubozu, morning/evening star. The rule: a pattern only matters _at a level, with volume_.
- **Do:** Scroll historical charts; mark and label 20 real patterns.
- **Milestone:** Screenshot & correctly label 10 patterns from live/recent charts.

### Week 4 - Support, resistance, supply & demand, and trend

- **Learn:** Support & resistance zones, trendlines, higher-highs/lower-lows, role reversal. Then step up to **supply & demand zones** - the tighter, higher-probability version that shows _where intraday moves begin_. Full lesson: [supply-and-demand.md](./supply-and-demand.md).
- **Do:** Mark S/R **and** fresh supply/demand zones (base + departure) on Nifty daily + 5-min. Note where price actually reacted.
- **Milestone:** Draw fresh demand & supply zones that price visibly respected - the base of every setup ahead.

## Month 2 - Tools, Setups & First Paper Trades

_Turn reading into a system._

### Week 5 - The four indicators you need

- **Learn:** EMA (9 / 20 / 50), VWAP (the institutional fair-value line), RSI, and volume. Ignore the other 200 - these are enough.
- **Do:** Add them to your chart. Watch how price behaves around VWAP all week.
- **Milestone:** Explain what "price above/below VWAP" tells you about intraday bias.

### Week 6 - Pick ONE setup: Opening Range Breakout (ORB)

- **Learn:** ORB, EMA crossover, VWAP bounce, breakout-and-retest. Then commit to _one_ - ORB - and write down exact entry, stop & target rules.
- **Do:** Manually backtest ORB across 10 past trading days. Tally wins/losses.
- **Milestone:** A one-paragraph written rulebook for ORB you can follow mechanically.

### Week 7 - Your first paper trades

- **Learn:** The full execution flow: placing entry, stop-loss and target orders correctly on your demo platform.
- **Do:** Paper-trade ORB _only_, max 1–2 trades/day. Journal every single one.
- **Milestone:** 10 journaled paper trades, each with a screenshot and a reason.

### Week 8 - Risk & position sizing

- **Learn:** The 1–2% rule, risk:reward ≥ 1:2, and the size formula: `shares = money risked ÷ (entry − stop)`.
- **Do:** Recalculate correct size for every paper trade before entering.
- **Milestone:** Zero trades placed without a pre-defined stop-loss and position size.

---

## Month 3 - Discipline, Review & Go-Live

_Prove the process, then trade small._

### Week 9 - Intensive paper trading

- **Learn:** How to add a second setup (VWAP bounce) without breaking your rules.
- **Do:** Place 20+ paper trades under strict rules. Track win rate and average R:R.
- **Milestone:** Journal shows real numbers: your win rate and average reward-per-risk.

### Week 10 - Psychology & your trading plan

- **Learn:** The account-killers: revenge trading, over-trading, FOMO. Set a fixed daily loss limit and a max-trades-per-day cap.
- **Do:** Write your one-page trading plan: setups, risk per trade, daily limits, when you stop.
- **Milestone:** A personal trading plan you actually follow for a full week.

### Week 11 - Review & refine

- **Learn:** Reading your own data - which setup, and which time window, actually works for you.
- **Do:** Analyse the full journal. Cut what loses; keep what wins.
- **Milestone:** A consistent, rule-following process - profit optional, discipline mandatory.

### Week 12 - Go live: small & real

- **Learn:** Real costs: brokerage, STT, GST, DP charges - and choosing capital you can genuinely afford to lose.
- **Do:** Fund a small account. Trade minimum size, 1 trade/day, same rules as paper.
- **Milestone:** Your first real intraday trade - placed with a plan, a stop, and a journal. **You're trading.**

---

## ⚠️ Non-Negotiable Risk Rules (from Week 1, forever)

Most beginners don't lose because their analysis is bad. They lose because they break these.

- A **stop-loss** on _every_ trade, decided before you enter.
- Risk **≤ 1–2%** of capital per trade. Never more.
- Only take trades with reward:risk **≥ 1:2**.
- A **daily loss limit** - hit it, and you're done for the day.
- Max **2–3 trades/day** while learning.
- Never **average down** a losing intraday position.

---

## Ready to Go Live? (end of Week 11 check)

Don't fund a real account until every box is honestly ticked.

- [ ] 40+ paper trades journaled with screenshots and reasons.
- [ ] A written trading plan with defined setups and daily limits.
- [ ] Position-sizing done automatically before each entry.
- [ ] You've followed your own rules for two straight weeks - no revenge/over-trading.
- [ ] Capital set aside that you can afford to lose entirely.
