# Daily Intraday Trading Routine

A repeatable daily workflow for your NSE intraday practice. Copy the **Daily Log** section into a new dated entry each trading day. Pairs with [roadmap.md](./roadmap.md) and its Non-Negotiable Risk Rules.

> ⚠️ Weeks 1–11 = paper trading only. This routine is the same whether you're on paper or (from Week 12) small real capital - that's the point.

---

## 🕐 The Daily Timeline (IST)

### Pre-market - 8:45–9:15 AM

- [ ] Check global cues: US markets (overnight), SGX Nifty / GIFT Nifty.

- [ ] Note any big news / results / events today (RBI, budget, major earnings).

- [ ] Mark yesterday's key levels on Nifty & Bank Nifty (high, low, close, S/R zones).

- [ ] Decide today's bias in one line: _bullish / bearish / neutral - why._

- [ ] Confirm your daily loss limit and max trades for today.

### Opening watch - 9:15–9:30 AM

- [ ] Do **not** trade the first 15 minutes. Just watch.

- [ ] Mark the **opening range**: high & low of the first 15-min candle.

### Active window - 9:30 AM–12:00 PM

- [ ] Wait for _your_ setup (e.g. ORB) at a _marked level_ with _volume_.

- [ ] Before every entry, write: entry, stop-loss, target, position size.

- [ ] Position size = money risked ÷ (entry − stop). Risk ≤ 1–2% of capital.

- [ ] Only take reward:risk ≥ 1:2. If it's not there, skip it.

### Midday - 12:00–2:00 PM

- [ ] Low-volume "chop" zone. Usually stand aside. Avoid boredom trades.

### Closing window - 2:00–3:20 PM

- [ ] Trend often resumes. Same rules - no new setups after 3:20.

- [ ] All intraday (MIS) positions must be closed before auto-square-off (\~3:20–3:25).

### Post-market - after 3:30 PM

- [ ] Fill in today's Daily Log (below) completely.

- [ ] Screenshot every trade's chart with entry/exit marked.

- [ ] Rate your **discipline**, not your profit.

---

## 🛑 Stop-Trading Triggers (any one = done for the day)

- Hit your daily loss limit.
- Reached max trades for the day (2–3 while learning).
- You feel angry, revengeful, or you're "trying to win it back." → Close the platform.

## Weekly Wrap (do every weekend)

- [ ] Count trades, wins, losses; compute win rate and average R:R.

- [ ] How many times did you break a rule? (Target: zero.)

- [ ] Which setup / time window performed best?

- [ ] Update your one-page trading plan if the data says so.

- [ ] Set the focus for next week from [roadmap.md](./roadmap.md).
